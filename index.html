About Us

厦门鸿识科技有限公司（Xiamen Hongshi Technology Co., LTD）成立于 2018 年，总部位于中国厦门。我们是一家专注于物联网（IoT）技术与泛建筑行业数字化转型的创新型科技企业。

依托多年的行业深耕，鸿识科技为工程项目提供涵盖"移动端 App、专有智能硬件（PDA）、云端 SaaS 平台"在内的软硬件一体化解决方案，助力企业实现劳务实名制合规、现场安全管控与考勤精准追踪。

Founded in 2018 and headquartered in Xiamen, China, Xiamen Hongshi Technology Co., LTD is an innovative tech enterprise focusing on Internet of Things (IoT) technology and the digital transformation of the construction industry. We provide comprehensive integrated solutions—including mobile applications (iOS/Android), specialized smart hardware (PDA), and cloud-based SaaS platforms—to help enterprises achieve compliance in real-name labor management and precise attendance tracking.
